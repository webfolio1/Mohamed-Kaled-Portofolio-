Andalus Restaurant Social Media Campaign

Complete social media strategy, copywriting, and scheduling for Instagram and Facebook

Project Overview

Andalus Restaurant required a comprehensive social media presence to engage their target audience and showcase their authentic Middle Eastern cuisine. I developed and executed a complete content strategy covering copywriting, creative content creation, and strategic scheduling across Meta platforms.

Platform

Instagram & Facebook

Duration

Ongoing Campaign

Content Type

Copywriting & Scheduling

Challenge

Create engaging, culturally relevant content that resonates with the target demographic while maintaining consistent posting schedules across multiple social platforms. The challenge was to balance promotional content with value-driven posts that build community engagement.

My Role & Solution

  • Content Strategy: Developed comprehensive content calendar aligned with restaurant events, seasonal offerings, and cultural occasions
  • Copywriting: Created compelling Arabic and English captions that tell brand stories and drive engagement
  • Scheduling: Implemented strategic posting schedule using Meta Business Suite for optimal reach and engagement
  • Visual Consistency: Maintained brand voice and visual identity across all posts
  • Community Management: Planned response strategies for comments and engagement
